# berlin-pizza-pricing

🍕 Pizza Pricing Strategy in Berlin
📌 Project Overview:

This project analyzes pizza pricing across restaurants in Berlin to determine where to open a new pizza place and how to price Margherita, Salami, and Funghi competitively.

🎯 Business Question:

Where should I open a pizza restaurant in Berlin and what pricing strategy should I use?

📊 Data:

3 pizza types: Margherita, Salami, Funghi

Features:
Price
Size
District
Central vs Non-Central
Italian vs Non-Italian
Rating
Review Count

🔎 Key Analysis:

Central vs Non-Central pricing

Authenticity (Italian) price premium

Rating vs pricing power

District-level opportunity analysis

📈 Key Findings:

Central locations charge higher prices

Italian branding adds a pricing premium

Higher ratings allow higher pricing

Some districts show opportunity gaps

🏆 Final Recommendation:

Open in a semi-central district with strong quality positioning and price slightly below central average.
